SURFACE CARGO

Our Company provides services for Full Truck Load (FTL) as well as Part Truck Load (PTL) to customers, which gives them flexibility for quantity of their consignment. Under PTL, the customers have the flexibility of hiring the truck on part basis. The goods of various customers are consolidated and transported to multiple destinations on the same truck. Under FTL, we provide door to door service wherein the entire truck is loaded with goods of a single customer and are delivered to the delivery point as specified by the customer.

AIR CARGO

Air Cargo Service is a fast transportation service and used by customers with urgent transportation need and / or perishable goods. Currently, we provide same-day-delivery service only for Metro cities in India. Our air consignments move through various airways. Air Cargo bookings are on requirement basis, and a network of various airlines ensures better availability of consignments. Air Cargo is beneficial to our customers as it is a time-definite mode and also beneficial to our Company as it helps in route optimization.
